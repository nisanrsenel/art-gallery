Art by



Habibe SenelYılmaz






about artist

She graduated from the Faculty of Theology and improved herself in this field. She continues her career as an educator.

The artist, who also likes to work with landscapes in her oil paintings, was influenced by the rich pattern repertoire containing various decorative ways and methods and geometric abstract units in Islamic Architecture and Islamic arts, and reflected this style in her works.

"Beauty, which is the essence of art, is a divine quality, and the artist is the one who discovers this beauty."
